About Me

"I'm not perfect, but I'm enough" - Carl Rogers.

"As your therapist, my focus is to offer you support while you heal from the distress or trauma that has motivated you to reach out. I am an EMDR therapist and provide care to people who have experienced trauma; be that one traumatic event, or multiple traumas over the course of your life. I also help people to clarify and achieve personal goals, develop self-awareness and self-compassion. I am registered with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario (CRPO) and have my Masters in Counselling Psychology from Yorkville University as well as a BA in Psychology from York University. People come to me who are experiencing PTSD, anxiety, trauma, depression, burnout, identity issues, parents looking for parenting support or navigating the transition to parenthood, grief and loss, perinatal and postnatal trauma, ADHD, Autism; and immigrants, refugees and newcomers to Canada who are struggling with their sense of belonging."
